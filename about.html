About SignCrafty

A focused signature generator plus plain-language guides—built so you can export and use your mark without friction.

What we do

SignCrafty helps you create a digital signature image you can drop into PDFs, emails, presentations, or design work. You can draw freehand with your mouse, trackpad, or touchscreen, or type your name and choose from elegant script styles. When you are ready, you download PNG, JPG, or (for drawn signatures) SVG—without creating an account.

Privacy by design

Signature creation happens in your browser. We built SignCrafty so you can generate and export your signature without uploading it to us for processing. That keeps your workflow simple and your file under your control.

Guides, not guesswork

Export formats and PDF workflows can be confusing. Our Guides explain how transparent PNGs work, how different file types compare, and how to think about browser-based tools safely—so the site is useful even when you are not using the generator.

Important limitation

SignCrafty produces a signature image. It is not the same as a regulated electronic signature platform used for certain legal contracts. Always confirm what your document or organization requires.

Privacy Policy

How SignCrafty collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website and tools.

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Overview

SignCrafty (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates this website and the free online signature tools available at SignCrafty. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you visit, how we use it, and your choices—especially regarding cookies and advertising.

Signature creation is processed in your browser

The SignCrafty signature generator runs locally in your web browser. When you draw or type a signature, that processing happens on your device. We do not require an account to use the tool, and we do not ask you to upload your signature image to our servers in order to generate or download your file.

Information we may collect

Depending on how you use the site, we or our partners may process:

  • Technical and usage data: such as approximate location (country or region), device type, browser type, pages viewed, and interactions—often collected automatically through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Communications: if you email us (for example through a contact address listed on the site), we receive your message content and email address.

Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies and related technologies (such as local storage or pixels) for essential site operation, preferences where applicable, analytics, and—where enabled—personalized advertising.

Advertising (Google AdSense)

This site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense or other Google advertising partners. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to show ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites, to measure ad performance, and to limit how often you see an ad.

You can learn more about how Google uses data when you use its partners’ sites or apps in Google’s policy documentation. You may opt out of personalized advertising from Google by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. You can also manage many third-party cookies through industry tools such as the Digital Advertising Alliance (United States) or your browser settings.

Third-party links

Our guides may reference third-party software or services (such as PDF readers). Those services have their own privacy policies, which we do not control.

Children’s privacy

SignCrafty is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us so we can delete it where appropriate.

International visitors

If you access the site from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate. Those transfers may be subject to different data protection laws.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the site after changes means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the details on our Contact page.
